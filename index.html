Autonomous Robotics & Embodied Intelligence Systems Lab (Khalifa University)
ARIES Lab is a research laboratory at Khalifa University, Abu Dhabi, dedicated to advancing the embodiment and real-world applications of robotic systems across AgriTech, Marine Technologies, Animal Conservation, and Robotic Manipulation. We focus on seamlessly integrating physical intelligence, encompassing actuation and sensing, with cognitive intelligence, comprising control, planning, and learning, within robotic systems. Our innovative solutions have found applications across a spectrum of domains, including industrial, healthcare, and marine robotics. Through our research endeavors, we strive to narrow gaps and enrich practical applications in these diverse fields. Our research in these areas have yielded notable contributions, documented in various patents and published papers. showcasing the impact of our research in these domains
