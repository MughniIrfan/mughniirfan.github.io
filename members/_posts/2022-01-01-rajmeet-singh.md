Rajmeet Singh completed his PhD in Robotics from Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala, India, in 2019.

Currently, he serves as a postdoctoral researcher at the Centre for Autonomous Robotic Systems (KUCARS) at Khalifa University, UAE. Previously, from 2022 to 2023, he held a postdoctoral researcher position at the University of Windsor, Ontario, Canada.

Since 2010, Dr. Singh has worked as an assistant professor in the Mechanical Engineering Department at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala, and BBSBEC College, India.

Dr. Singh has authored three book chapters and over 30 articles. His research interests include mobile robotics, visual servoing, reinforcement learning, visual odometry, software-hardware integration, and controls.
