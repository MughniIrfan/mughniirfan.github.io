Muhammad Owais is a Postdoctoral Fellow in Mechanical & Nuclear Engineering at Khalifa University.

He received his PhD in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from Dongguk University, Seoul, Republic of South Korea, in 2022. His KU profile highlights work in AI methods for image and pattern recognition, explainable AI in medical imaging, and computer vision applications.

His research interests include AI for medical diagnostics, multidimensional biomedical imaging analysis, X-ray imagery analysis for threat detection, and AI applications in industrial robotics.
