Waseem Akram received the BCS degree (Hons.) from Hazara University Mansehra, the MSCS degree from COMSATS University Islamabad, Pakistan, and a PhD in ICT from the University of Calabria, Italy.

He is currently a postdoctoral research fellow at Khalifa University, UAE. His research interests include marine robotics, control and navigation, vision-based control, and autonomous detection and recognition.
