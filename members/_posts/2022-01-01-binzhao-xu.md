Binzhao Xu is a researcher affiliated with Mechanical & Nuclear Engineering at Khalifa University.

His KU research records include work on reinforcement learning for dynamic robotic grasping and learning-based manipulation in unstructured environments. His KU doctoral thesis, "AI Driven Robotic Manipulation," focuses on online adaptive learning systems for manipulation in unseen and dynamic settings.

His research interests include robotic manipulation, reinforcement learning, trajectory prediction, and imitation learning.
