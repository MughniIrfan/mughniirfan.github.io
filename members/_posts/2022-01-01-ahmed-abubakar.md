Ahmed Abubakar (listed as Ahmad Abubakar on KU research records) is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Khalifa University.

His KU doctoral thesis, "Traction Awareness and Control Through Haptic Teleoperation of Space Rovers," focuses on high-fidelity bilateral teleoperation, traction-aware control, and delay compensation for rover operation on soft terrains.

His research interests include haptic teleoperation, predictive control for delayed systems, traction/slip estimation, and autonomous mobility for lunar and space rover applications.
