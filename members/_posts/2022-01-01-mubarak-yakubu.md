Mubarak Yakubu is a Postdoctoral Fellow at Khalifa University.

His KU doctoral thesis, "Towards Autonomous Lunar Exploration: A Learning-based Lunar Rover Mobility System," addresses adaptive rover mobility, slip estimation, and navigation on deformable and heterogeneous terrains.

His research interests include planetary rover mobility systems, soft-terrain locomotion, teleoperation under communication delay, and autonomous navigation for space robotics.
